Aberdeen Business School

Home to the Georgina Scott Sutherland Study Centre, providing students with a private study area with excellent IT facilities. It also houses 'Moot Court' for Law & Applied Social Studies students as well as the TV & radio studios, all providing students with real life learning experiences.
Opened on our Garthdee campus in 1999, Aberdeen Business School provides first class formal & informal learning spaces. Our wide ranging portfolio of business, management & accounting degree programmes are taught both online & in the school's fantastic campus.
